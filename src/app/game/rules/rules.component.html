Game Rules

Goal:The goal of the game is to find which island has the greatest average height.

How to Play:

  • You are presented with a 30x30 grid map. Each cell represents either water (height = 0) or land (height > 0).
  • Connected land cells form an island.
  • Click on any island to make a guess. You have 3 attempts to find the correct island.
  • After 3 wrong guesses or a successful guess, the game ends.
  • You will have helpers like the compass or different colored islands after your pick, don't forget to check your lives at the top left.
